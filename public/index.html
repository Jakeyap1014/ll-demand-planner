Forecast Accuracy — How It Works

What is WMAPE?

Weighted Mean Absolute Percentage Error measures how accurate demand forecasts are. Lower = more predictable.

How We Forecast

4-week rolling average: Forecast(this week) = avg of actual sales from the previous 4 weeks.

Forecast = (Week-4 + Week-3 + Week-2 + Week-1) / 4

WMAPE Formula

WMAPE = Sum(|Forecast - Actual|) / Sum(Actual) x 100%

How to Read Results

WMAPERatingMeaning
0-30%GoodDemand predictable. Trust reorder calculations.
31-50%FairSome variability. Add extra safety stock.
50%+PoorErratic demand. Plan manually with larger buffers.

What Causes High WMAPE?

Promotions/sales events, stockouts (zero sales = not demand), new products with no history, seasonal shifts, viral social media spikes.

Data Source

Shopify order history (90 days), grouped by ISO week. First 4 weeks = seed. WMAPE evaluated from week 5 onwards.

Limitations

Simple moving average (no trend/seasonality adjustment). 90-day window = ~9 weeks of evaluation. Stockout periods artificially improve WMAPE.
